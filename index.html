A Trusted Resource

In a Judicial Foreclosure state such as Florida, the first publicly recorded step of the foreclosure process is a Lis Pendens filed in the public records of the same county where the property is located.

This legal notice signals the beginning of litigation affecting title to real property. For investors, agents, lenders, and owners, accessing this data early creates opportunity.

We provide fast and reliable Florida Lis Pendens filing data to real estate professionals across all 67 Florida counties.
